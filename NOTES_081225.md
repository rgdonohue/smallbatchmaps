## 2) Services section

**Issue:** Copy leans clever (“do their damn job,” “no black boxes”) but stays abstract. Buyers want deliverables, timeframes, and artifacts.

**Fix pattern (for each card):** Outcome sentence → 3 bullets with deliverables → proof link.

**Samples (tight, concrete):**

  *Turn messy, multi-source data into decision-ready layers and notebooks.*
  **Deliverables:** cleaned & documented datasets (GeoParquet/GeoPackage), reproducible Jupyter workflow, QA/QC report with assumptions & limitations.
  **Typical timeline:** 2–4 weeks for first insights.
* **Web Cartography**
  *Ship fast, accessible, client-side maps with beautiful, purposeful styles.*
  **Deliverables:** PMTiles/MBTiles + MapLibre style, performance budget + Lighthouse/Map timing, usage docs.
  **Options:** static tiles (Jamstack) or lightweight API.
* **AI-Assisted Workflows**
  *Use LLMs where they actually help—testing, docs, style scaffolds—not as magic.*
  **Deliverables:** test suite stubs, schema/style generators, refactor PRs with before/after diffs.
  **Guardrails:** privacy, eval harness, failure modes documented.
